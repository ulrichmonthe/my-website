Notable Past Projects

The First Project

Supplier Management Portal (SMP) is a cloud-based platform designed to simplify and optimize procurement and supplier management processes. It connects large-scale buyers with local micro, small, and medium-sized enterprises (MSMEs), helping multinational companies diversify their supply chains and access new markets. The portal allows buyers to easily find and vet local suppliers, while suppliers gain exposure to new business opportunities with larger corporations.

The Second Project

Catchafire is a New York-based organization which makes skill-based connections between professional volunteers and other non-profits. It was founded in 2009 by Rachael Chong. Catchafire matches professionals who want to donate their time with nonprofits who need their skills.
